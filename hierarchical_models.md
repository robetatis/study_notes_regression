## Starting point: the multiple linear model

The response is $y = \langle y_1, y_2, ..., y_n \rangle^T$, which is continuous.

We have a **fixed** design matrix $\mathbf{X}$:

$$
\mathbf{X} =
\begin{bmatrix}
v_1 \\
v_2 \\
v_3
\end{bmatrix}
$$
